🎙️
High-pass your vocals
Cut everything below 80–100Hz on your vocal track. You don't need it and it's competing with your 808 and kick for no reason.
🎚️
Compress harder than you think
Rap vocals need heavy compression to sit in a busy beat. Start at 4:1 ratio with 6–8dB of gain reduction. If it sounds over-compressed, you're getting close.
🔊
Add saturation for body
Thin or tinny vocals are usually missing harmonic content. A subtle saturator adds warmth in the 1–3kHz range that makes vocals feel full without touching EQ.
